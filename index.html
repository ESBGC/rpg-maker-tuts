1: Intro to RPG Maker VX Ace

What is RPG Maker?

Do you love RPG games like Pokemon, Legend of Zelda, or Final Fantasy? RPG Maker VX Ace is a game engine that anybody can use to make 2D roleplaying games just like this. You don’t need to know programming to make a great, fun, interesting and professional RPG---you can just point and click! But, if you do know programming, you can customize your game even more.

These tutorials---adapted from the official documentation---will walk you through all the steps to make your very own RPG game. Let's dive in!

The Database

The database is really important: this is where you create, edit and organize most of the stuff that goes into your game, like characters, items, classes, enemies.... It’s kind of like the library for your RPG; as you build your game, you’ll check items out of your database.

What’s in the database?

Glad you asked:

Actors
The actors are the characters in your RPG. Though this isn’t where you design them from scratch, it is where you choose their design, as well as setting their stats, skills and description.
Classes
Classes are character types: in a typical RPG, your characters might be wizards, hunters or knights, and so on. Or, if you’re making an RPG about school, they could be teachers or teenagers, friends or bullies, classmates or new kids, or whatever other class of character you might find at school. A character’s class determines how fast they level up and what skills they learn.
